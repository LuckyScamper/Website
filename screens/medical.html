Medical (part of Marines)

The medical teams are part of the marines and fall under their command, with the exception of groundside colonial hospitals, who fall under colonial command.

Evo Medics are tasked with saving downed allies, even their own, and prevent their deaths. Medics work under extreme circumstances and their discipline and determination is what keeps Evo alive.

Under the leadership of Dr.Raptor-Vi, the medics have shown themselves to be Evo's last line of defense, even capable of picking up a weapon and shooting the enemy if necessary.As long as the medics stand, Evo will live.
