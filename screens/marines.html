EVO Marine Division

The Marine Division is Evo Corporation's on-ship and planetary fighting force. Trained for operations in the atmosphere, the vast vacuum of space, and on the decks of enemy vessels, the Marines, along with the Space Wing, serve as Evo's line of defense against those who would disrupt its operations, and its offensive arm against pirates and other such enemies in regards to boots on the ground and in space operations. The general infantry of the division is organized into the Infantry Corps. Its reconnaissance and intelligence arm is known as Force Reconnaissance. The Marine Division is also responsible for the medical needs of Evo with its Medical Corps.

Marine corpsmen and medical officers provides aid to both the Marines in combat and the rest of the corporation whenever needed.

While an infantry force, the Marine Division mans ground vehicles as well as close air support ships, small-medium transport ships, landing ships, and medical ships via its Marine Transport and Assault Vehicles Corps, or MTAV. The normal duties of the Marine Division include protecting Space Wing vessels, traveling alongside the Economic and Research & Development divisions to provide security for their own operations, and performing assaults on enemy entities, often alongside the Space Wing.

The Marine Division is headed by the Commandant, a general (O-7), who is assisted by the Vice Commandant, a lieutenant general (O-6).

It has a further, robust chain of command from its subdivisions to small fireteams.
