The Fleet

The Evo Corp Fleet consists of a multitude of Idrises, a Javelin, a Pioneer, a Polaris, a Starfarer, a Vulture, a number of Caterpillars, Hulls and many more.

These ships form the backbone of the Evo Corp, allowing them to conduct their business anywhere in the galaxy. None of their operations would be possible without the support of both the military and economic fleets.

Traveling through space can take a long time - sometimes days at an end. Are you ready to take on an adventure through space, not knowing what lies beyond and do work in these uncharted regions? Join the EVO Fleet today!
